The piano is an acoustic, stringed musical instrument,in which the strings are struck by hammers. It is played using a keyboard, which is a row of keys that the performer presses down or strikes with the fingers and thumbs of both hands to cause the hammers to strike the strings.

The piano was invented around the year 1700.

The piano was invented by by Bartolomeo Cristofori.

The piano was invented in Italy.

The piano was able to play both loudly and softly while the instruments at the time like the harpsichord or the clavichord were unable to do so consistently.
